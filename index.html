About Me

Hi, I’m Erik Herberg, a recent graduate student from the University of Washington, aspiring community leader, and a lifelong learner. I have a dynamic background ranging from graphic design to educating professionals about social issues in higher education. My interests include educational leadership, community outreach, social justice, marketing, and computer sciences.

As such, I have always strived to be knowledgeable in as many areas as possible as a way to spark innovation by combining different skillsets to seek out creative solutions to today’s changing social and economic climate. I am currently seeking a position where I can work with others and have the opportunity to demonstrate my abilities of creative problem solving, and social and economic awareness.

Thank you for visiting my site. If you have any questions about my work, please feel free to email me at erik@eaherberg.com.
